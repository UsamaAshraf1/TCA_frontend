TCA Approach

Case Study

Cavendish Farms

Industry: Agriculture & Food Processing
The Change Agency led a comprehensive change management strategy to transition the client's supply chain operations from a manual, labor-intensive process to a fully automated system. This transformation involved redefining workflows, integrating advanced technology, and ensuring seamless employee adoption.

Irving Oil

Industry: Oil & Gas
The Change Agency played a pivotal role in driving the successful implementation of Irving Oil’s Renewable Gas Program, a key component of their Energy Transition Program. Taking on a comprehensive change management leadership role, The Change Agency focused on ensuring the smooth introduction and integration of renewable natural gas (RNG) into the company's operations.
