How it Works

Step 1

Who Can Participate?

  • Business Associates
  • Past & Current Clients
  • Partners & Networking Connections
  • Employees or Associates
  • Change Management Advocates

Step 2

Who Should You Refer?

  • Businesses adopting AI or automation
  • Companies changing sales or customer strategies
  • Organizations going through mergers or leadership changes
  • Teams adapting to new markets
  • Leaders managing hybrid/remote transitions

Step 3

Know someone who could use our support?

  • Submit a referral using our referral form here: [Link]
  • Introduce us via LinkedIn or tag us in a relevant discussion
  • Send an email connecting us to your referral at: rylan@the-change-agency.ca

Step 4

We take it from there.

We’ll reach out within 48 hours, learn more about their needs, and offer a free Change Readiness Assessment to start their journey

Step 5

Stay in the loop

You’ll be updated as your referral moves through the pipeline from first contact to conversion
